Why Enroll with Us?

1. Live Classes
Live classes are offered to students in grades Pre-K to 12 four days a week.
Our students experience a real classroom setting collaborated with their teacher and students

2. Self Paced
self-paced learning is offered for grades 1-4. students can work at their own pace with the
guidance of the Learning Coach.
Self-paced students do not attend live classes and are guided by their Learning Coach. Teachers
offer support when requested for further assistance with the course material.
Self-paced students can enroll in religious studies courses for free.
Self-paced students are welcome to school-wide events, and programs.
Academics
Our students have access to their digital curriculum accounts, from where they have access to
their courses. This account is what the student
needs to complete his coursework and assessments.
Teachers make the utmost effort to ensure that-concepts that go against Islamic beliefs are not
taught in our lessons. Our curriculum is a pre-designed digital platform, therefore parents should
be aware that the course-ware includes concepts that go against Islamic beliefs. Teachers will
point out before signing homework when the student should take caution and at times skip a
particular section found in the lesson.

3. Annual Testing
We administer assessments to measure our student's learning. We use diagnostic assessments
to measure our student's learning against their peers.

About Us

Committed To Fun Filled Education.

Everyday Tarbiytul ilm Academy is a private virtual school serving students in preschool to 11th grade living around the world. TIAG is registered with the California Department of Education and has been fully Accreditation by Cognia with NCA, CASI, NWAC and SACS CASI.

We offer a comprehensive Academic Curriculum incorporating authentic Islamic Studies, Quran and Arabic instruction. Classes are safely taught by certified live instructors from the comfort and convenience of your home.

Our newly added Middle and High School program provides instruction for competency in skills needed to successfully complete requirements leading towards a U.S. HS Diploma.
