Dear Parents/Guardians: We hope you and your family had a wonderful summer, Therefore, we'd like to take this opportunity to extend a warm welcome to you and all new and returning students. As always, we are very excited and are anticipating another fantastic school year.

First day of school for students will be Wednesday. September 11, 2024.

Virtual Open House for parents will be held on September 4, 2024. It will offer an opportunity for you to meet your child/ren's teachers, discuss our e-Learning program, and review schedules and curriculum. You may also register your child/ren, at this. Please mark your calendar and plan on attending.

As you know, the beginning of the school year is a very busy time for us, however, our staff will be here to assist you in any way possible.

With that, again we'd like to thank you and we hope to serve the spiritual and academic needs of your family for another year.

Special Note - We are requesting all parents to please register online, if at all possible.

Please make note of the following prerequisites before your child/ren can begin school.

  • New students must have birth certificate and shot records.
  • Any tuition or fees from the past school year must be paid.
  • Tuition, registration fees, and down payment must be paid upon registration or on/or before the first day of school. Fees may be paid online via Zelle.
  • New students must have a report card or transcript from their previous school.
  • All students must be in proper uniform on their first day of school. (See Secretary for information)
  • Student textbook fees should be paid at the time of registration in order for your child to receive his/her books in a timely manner.

Your child will receive a supply list. Please make sure that these supplies are obtained promptly.
