HISTORY OF

TARBIYATUL ILM ACADEMY GLOBAL

Tarbiyatul Ilm Academy was founded in 2007 by professional educator as a home school alternative (and separate Family Daycare Home) using the Agora Cyber Charter School curriculum which was started with only three students in the living room of her home. The mission of the program was to provide quality education in an Islamic environment for parents' that were displeased with the Philadelphia Public School System.
As word spread about the program, it grew exponentially. TIA quickly outgrew its living space and began to take on a life of
its own.
By the 2008 – 2009 academic year, enrollment was at 20 students and the program was forced to move to a two-room apartment and commercial space for the daycare. Along with the Agora Cyber Charter School curriculum, TIA began to offer Quran and Islamic Studies as well. The Islamic Studies portion consisted of beginner’s Arabic and beneficial reminders from the more popular books of the Salaf. With growing success came the need for a larger venue, thus the search began to accommodate the needs of the growing enrollment.
With nearly 40 students, the school moved to a bigger location. In just two years, the home School Program grew in enrollment from 40 to 55 students (Daycare grew also)! To this date, the program has remained intact, changing its status to a Non-Public/Private Religious Registered school with the Pennsylvania Department of Education while keeping all its students enrolled. Alhamdulilah!
The 2017 – 2018 academic year began with an enrollment of over 70 students at in the Germantown section of Philadelphia, A, bi’ithnillah, ending with an enrollment of over 125 students in a 40,000sq ft closed school building. Due to work and order to close our school doors. After 2022, We have worked diligently to enhance our curriculum and refocus on excelling and expanding in our program. To date, we have launched our Online platform and Tutoring Services which has gone Global!
